About Us

Our History

Plan-It Plumbing, Heating & Air was founded in 1980 by Jack Torrance and his wife, Wendy.

During a recent stint as a caretaker for the historic Overlook Hotel, Jack and Wendy became increasingly familiar with the upkeep and maintenance of the hotels plumbing, heating, and air conditioning units.

After relocating to San Diego, that experience led the couple to their tireless passion of providing the absolute best plumbing, heating, and air conditioning services possible.

Plan-It Plumbing, Heating & Air serves the entirety of San Diego County with the most experienced professionals, the latest technology, and quality service at an affordable price.
